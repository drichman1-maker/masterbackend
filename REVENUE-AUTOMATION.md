## Revenue Opportunities to Automate

### 1. Retail Arbitrage Alerts
**What:** Price differences between retailers
**Sources:** Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Target, Best Buy
**Signal:** Buy low at Retailer A → Sell high at Retailer B
**Action:** Email alerts with profit margin calc
**Frequency:** Every 2 hours

### 2. Clearance Deal Scraping
**What:** End-of-season clearance items
**Sources:** Home Depot, Lowe's, Macy's, REI
**Signal:** 50%+ off retail with resale value
**Action:** Slack/email alerts
**Frequency:** Daily at 6 AM

### 3. Price Mistake Detection
**What:** Obvious pricing errors
**Signal:** Price < 30% of market value
**Action:** Immediate SMS/email (time sensitive)
**Frequency:** Real-time monitoring

### 4. Flippable Inventory Alerts
**What:** Items with consistent resale demand
**Categories:** Limited sneakers, electronics, collectibles
**Signal:** Available at retail with resale premium
**Action:** Daily digest
**Frequency:** Once daily

### 5. Local Arbitrage (Craigslist/Facebook)
**What:** Underpriced local items
**Signal:** Price < 50% of eBay sold comps
**Action:** Alert with comp data
**Frequency:** 4x daily
