# App Design Systems & UI Reference

## Screenshot Analysis (Feb 15, 2026)

---

## 1. HandymanPaint / TaskBidder
**Type:** Contractor marketplace app
**Screens:** Login, Dashboard, Bidding interface

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Light blue (#87CEEB or similar sky blue)
- **Secondary:** White/cream backgrounds
- **Accent:** Gold/brown for rankings (1st, 2nd, 3rd place badges)
- **Text:** Dark gray/black
- **CTA:** Blue buttons (#2196F3 or similar)

### UI Style
- Clean, professional, trustworthy
- Card-based layout
- Bottom navigation (Home, Bids, Jobs, Messages, Profile)
- Stats cards with large numbers ($34,500, 92%)

### Logo Concept
- Paint roller + hammer combination (as seen in screenshot)
- Simple line art style
- Blue color to match primary

---

## 2. SoundCheck / LowKeyMode
**Type:** Sound measurement & wellness app
**Screens:** Sound measurement active, Low exposure display

### Color Palette (Active Measurement)
- **Background:** Deep navy blue gradient (#0A1628 to #1E3A5F)
- **Primary accent:** Cyan/electric blue (#00D4FF)
- **Score display:** White with glow effect
- **Status text:** Yellow/gold (#FFD700) for "Moderate exposure"
- **Buttons:** Red for stop (#FF4444), Blue for reset

### Color Palette (Low Exposure)
- **Background:** Purple gradient (#2D1B4E to #1A0F2E)
- **Score:** White with purple glow
- **Status:** Green (#00FF88) for "Low exposure"
- **Buttons:** Blue (#2196F3) for start

### UI Style
- Dark mode exclusive
- Circular progress indicators
- Minimalist, focused
- Typography: Clean sans-serif, large numerals

### Logo Concept
- Sound wave + checkmark
- Or circular dial/gauge icon
- Cyan/blue gradient

---

## 3. RetroRefine / RumbleDeals
**Type:** Video game price aggregator
**Screens:** Retro game grid, Modern console listings, Product detail

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep purple (#6B46C1)
- **Secondary:** Electric blue/cyan (#00D9FF)
- **Accent:** Pink/magenta (#FF00AA)
- **Background:** Dark purple gradient with glassmorphism
- **Cards:** Semi-transparent white/gray with blur
- **Retailer badges:** Color-coded (eBay blue, Amazon orange, GameStop red)

### UI Style
- Glassmorphism (frosted glass effect)
- Toggle switch for Retro/Modern modes
- Game cartridge aesthetic for retro
- Console product images for modern
- Price comparison tables

### Logo Concept
- Game controller + magnifying glass
- Or retro game cartridge with price tag
- Purple + cyan gradient

## 4. PokeAggregator / MintCondition (Pokemon)
**Type:** Pokemon card price tracker
**Screens:** Card detail, Market stats, Listings

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Light gray/white (#F5F5F7)
- **Primary:** Yellow/gold (#FFD700) - Pokemon brand color
- **Secondary:** Blue for CTAs (#2196F3)
- **Accent:** Green for positive/deals (#4CAF50)
- **Cards:** White with subtle shadows
- **Market data:** Black text with red/green indicators

### UI Style
- Light theme (clean, collectible focus)
- Large card images
- Market stats prominently displayed
- Ticker/price tables
- "Steal" badges for good deals

### Logo Concept
- Pokeball + price tag
- Or card with magnifying glass
- Yellow primary with blue accent

## 5. Aurum Wellness / Med-Device
**Type:** Medical/wellness equipment B2B site
**Screens:** Cryotherapy chambers, Recovery systems comparison, Homepage

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Pure black (#000000)
- **Primary accent:** Neon cyan (#00FFFF)
- **Secondary:** Teal/cyan gradient
- **Text:** White for headings, cyan for highlights
- **Cards:** Semi-transparent dark with cyan borders
- **CTA:** Cyan buttons with glow effect

### UI Style
- Clinical, premium, futuristic
- Dark mode exclusive
- Glowing cyan accents
- Comparison tables with data
- Medical device imagery
- B2B professional aesthetic

### Logo Concept
- Abstract human figure + pulse/energy wave
- Or stylized "A" with medical cross
- Cyan neon glow effect

## 6. Hardware Intelligence / MacTrackr iOS
**Type:** Apple product price tracking app
**Screens:** Browse devices, Product detail, Price alerts

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Dark gray (#1C1C1E) to black gradient
- **Primary:** Cyan blue (#00D4FF)
- **Secondary:** Light gray cards (#2C2C2E)
- **Success:** Green (#34C759) for deals/best price
- **Alert:** Yellow/amber (#FF9500) for paused monitoring
- **Text:** White headings, gray secondary

### UI Style
- Dark mode exclusive
- Hardware product cards with specs
- Price comparison with retailers
- Toggle switches for alerts
- "BUY NOW" prominent for good deals
- Bottom tab navigation

### Logo Concept
- Apple silhouette + circuit lines
- Or magnifying glass + Apple logo
- Tech-forward, premium feel

## Unified Brand Architecture

### Parent Brand: CodeMode
**Concept:** Studio/umbrella brand for all apps
**Color:** Dark navy (#0A1628) with cyan accent (#00D4FF)
**Logo:** Abstract code brackets or CM monogram

### Sub-Brands:

| App | Primary Color | Secondary | Mood |
|-----|---------------|-----------|------|
| LowKeyMode | Navy/Cyan | Purple | Calm, tech, wellness |
| HealthIndex | Teal/Green | White | Health, clean, fresh |
| MacTrackr | Apple-style gray | Blue | Tech, premium, Apple |
| MintCondition | Gold/Yellow | Blue | Collectibles, value |
| GrailWatch | Purple | Pink | Retro, gaming, fun |
| Appliance Scout | Orange/Red | White | Home, practical |
| Fixed Income | Navy/Gold | White | Finance, trust |
| TaskBidder | Sky Blue | Brown | Trust, work, service |

### Typography Recommendations

**Headings:**
- Inter (modern, clean)
- SF Pro Display (Apple ecosystem)

**Body:**
- Inter or SF Pro Text
- Roboto (Android/web)

**Numbers/Data:**
- SF Mono or JetBrains Mono (tabular figures)
