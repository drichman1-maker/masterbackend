## Why AWIN?

AWIN provides access to merchants not available through Amazon Associates:
- Specialized health device manufacturers
- Independent Apple resellers
- Collectibles dealers and auction houses
- Gaming retailers with exclusive deals

This diversification reduces dependency on single-platform revenue.
