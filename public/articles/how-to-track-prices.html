How to Track Apple Product Prices & Never Miss a Deal

Why Track Prices?

Apple products rarely go on sale, but when they do, the savings are significant. A MacBook Pro can drop $300-500 during sales events.

Best Times to Buy

  • Black Friday/Cyber Monday: 10-20% off
  • Back to School (July-Aug): Free AirPods with Mac/iPad
  • Spring (March-Apr): Previous-gen discounts
  • Refurb Store: 10-15% off year-round
Pro Tip: Use price history charts to identify the best time to buy. Prices typically drop 3-6 months after launch.
