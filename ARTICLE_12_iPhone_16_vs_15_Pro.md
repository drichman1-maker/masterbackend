## The $200 Question

With the iPhone 16 Pro starting at $999 and the iPhone 15 Pro now $799, is the newer model worth the premium? Let's break down the real differences.

## Key Upgrades in iPhone 16 Pro

### Camera System
- **48MP Ultra Wide** (up from 12MP) — Major improvement for macro shots
- **5x Optical Zoom** on both Pro models — Previously only on Pro Max
- **Camera Control button** — Physical shutter/zoom control
- **4K 120fps Dolby Vision** — Cinematic slow-motion

### Performance
- **A18 Pro chip** — 20% faster CPU, 40% faster GPU
- **16-core Neural Engine** — 35% faster AI processing
- **8GB RAM** — Same as 15 Pro, but better optimized

### Display & Design
- **Larger 6.3" display** (up from 6.1") — More screen, barely bigger body
- **Thinner bezels** — 1.15mm vs 1.55mm
- **Grade 5 Titanium** — Stronger, lighter than aluminum

### Battery Life
- **27 hours video playback** — 4 hours more than 15 Pro
- **25W MagSafe charging** — Faster wireless charging

## What Hasn't Changed

- 120Hz ProMotion display
- Dynamic Island
- USB-C port
- Action Button (new to both in 2024)
- Face ID
- Ceramic Shield front
