# Pokemon Card Grading: PSA vs BGS vs CGC (2026 Guide)

**Meta Description:** "Confused about Pokemon card grading? Compare PSA, BGS, and CGC grading services to find the best option for your collection."

## Why Grade Pokemon Cards?
Grading protects your investment by authenticating and assessing card condition, which directly impacts resale value. High grades can multiply a card’s worth.

## PSA Overview
**Pros:**
- Industry standard for resale value.
- Largest population reports for comparison.
**Cons:**
- Higher costs and slower turnaround times.
- No subgrades (just an overall score).
**Cost:** $25–$300 per card, depending on service level.
**Turnaround:** 2–6 months.

## BGS Overview
**Pros:**
- Subgrades (4 categories: corners, edges, surface, centering).
- "Black Label" 10s command premium prices.
**Cons:**
- Less liquid than PSA for lower grades.
- Strict grading standards.
**Cost:** $30–$350 per card.
**Turnaround:** 1–4 months.

## CGC Overview
**Pros:**
- Faster turnaround times.
- Lower costs for bulk submissions.
- Newer player with growing acceptance.
**Cons:**
- Lower resale value compared to PSA/BGS.
- Less historical data for collectors.
**Cost:** $15–$200 per card.
**Turnaround:** 2–8 weeks.

## Cost Comparison Table
| Service | Base Cost | Subgrades | Turnaround |
|---------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| PSA     | $25       | No        | 2–6 months |
| BGS     | $30       | Yes       | 1–4 months |

## Which to Choose?
- **Resale Focus:** PSA or BGS (premium grades).
- **Budget/Casual:** CGC (faster and cheaper).
- **Subgrades Matter:** BGS.
